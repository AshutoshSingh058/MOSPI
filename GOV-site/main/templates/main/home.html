About HCES

The Household Consumption Expenditure Survey (HCES) is designed to collect information on consumption of goods and services by the households. The information collected in HCES helps to understand the consumption and expenditure pattern, standard of living and well-being of the households. The National Sample Survey Office has been conducting this survey since its inception in 1950. The survey of 2022-23 is the latest one on the subject. The current survey (HCES: 2022-23) has been conducted during the period August, 2022 to July, 2023 in whole of the Indian Union except a few inaccessible villages in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.
